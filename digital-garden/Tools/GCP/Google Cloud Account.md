An account in the [[Google Cloud Platform]] is linked to a Google Account.

The Google Account may be added to an organization in GCP. Example:

```
Google Account
└─┬ Personal Project 0.1
  ├─┬ Organization 1
  | ├── Project 1.1
  | ├── Project 1.2
  | └── …
  └─┬ Organization 2
    ├── Project 2.1
    └── …
```
